Academic life and goals

I am a Master's student in Mathematics at the Universidad Industrial de Santander. My main interests lie in topology, descriptive set theory, and their connections to analysis. I am also deeply interested in functional analysis and differential geometry. I aim to pursue a Ph.D. in Mathematics and continue doing research abroad, focusing on topology and descriptive set theory.
